Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Blocksmith.tools respects your privacy. We do not collect, store or sell any personal data. You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use our tools. Everything runs directly in your browser.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage only to remember small preferences, such as when you last submitted a bug report and whether you have dismissed certain notices. We do not set any tracking cookies of our own.

Advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense to keep the site free. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information about how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

Third Party Services

We use Formspree to process the contact and bug report forms. Any information you type into those forms is sent to us through Formspree. See Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Block textures are loaded from public Minecraft asset content delivery networks for display purposes only.

Analytics

We may use privacy friendly analytics to understand general traffic patterns, such as how many people visit and which tools are most popular. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Children's Privacy

Blocksmith Tools is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since we do not collect personal data at all, there is nothing for us to store or share.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last changed. Continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out via the contact page or email help@blocksmith.tools.
